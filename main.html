About Me

I am a Software Developer

Hello, I am Kaitlynn Wilhite, a software developer. Although that wasn't the case until very recently. I started out, like many do, at McDonalds. I worked in customer service for five years, across many different spectrums of the term. I had then grown weary of it and moved to child care. I love it so much really, but it wasn't enough to live on. After I went to the industrial industry, I told myself as soon as I was hired that it was only temporary until I decided on a career path I wanted to follow. I quickly lost sight of my future, I was finally making it on my own, so I stayed there for two years.
After months of feeling the wear that manual labor puts on your body, I finally started looking for what I wanted out of life. I started fueling my creative side, started painting, researching careers that may interest me. This last Christmas I was given the opportunity to give back to my community and paint a mural on a local shop front on our main square. It was one of the funnest things I have ever done! Displaying my handy work for my hometown to see was absolutely amazing, and this is what really got me on my path for my future!
After a few months of self exploring I finally realized I was truly a creator, ready to put my new found confidence to any task. I had looked into Eleven Fifty Coding Academy before starting in a factory setting, and passed on the opportunity due to financial reasons. I cannot tell you how much I kick myself for not going sooner as an ElevenFifty Alumni now.
